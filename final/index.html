About

I am an undergraduate CS student at the University of Pittsburgh, expected to graduate May 2018.

As a sentient rubber duck, I've faced adversity at every turn in my life. I don't know how I was born; I can only imagine I was cooked up in some lab experiment gone sideways. At any rate, I have been orphaned for as long as I remember. I brought myself up with nothing but the light of the moon and The C Programming Language to guide me.

I have a wealth of experience in the latest web technologies, including HTML and CSS, as evidenced by this very page!

I'd ask you not to worry about details like how I use a keyboard; instead, consider what I can offer your company.
